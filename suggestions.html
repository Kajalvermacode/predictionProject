Health Recommendations

🔴 High Risk Suggestions

  • Doctor se turant consult karein aur HbA1c test karwayein.
  • Sugar aur Meethi cheezein (Sweets, Cold drinks) bilkul band kar dein.
  • Daily 30 mins brisk walking (tez chalna) shuru karein.
  • Khaane mein Fiber (Saag, Salad) zyada shamil karein.

🟠 Moderate Risk Suggestions

  • Processed food aur maida khana kam karein.
  • Apna wazan (Weight) control mein rakhne ki koshish karein.
  • Har 3 mahine mein sugar level check karwayein.
  • Stress kam karein aur achhi neend lein.

🟢 Normal / Healthy Suggestions

  • Balanced diet lete rahein.
  • Active lifestyle barkaraar rakhein.
  • Saal mein ek baar regular body checkup karwayein.
  • Paani khoob peeyein.
